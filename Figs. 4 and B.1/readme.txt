Scripts to replicate Figs. 4 and B.1
	The data (10 datasets) are generated by n_gamma_crate_data.py and n_mixtureG_create_data.py
	The scripts n_gamma_experiment.py and n_mixtureG_experiment.py apply various estimation methods to these generated datasets and save the results.
	plot_n_sample_experiments_main_text.py produces Fig. 4
	plot_n_sample_experiments_appendix.py produces Fig. B.1
